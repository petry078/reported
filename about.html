Reported.space

Reported.space is a simple, fast, dark-moded, distraction free note-taking web app that autosave your work locally on your browser. The text is stored on client browser with JavaScript localStorage property.

It works really fast, it is just a few lines of code.

Can be used with Grammarly, LanguageTool and others browser spell checkers.
